1.1.1 Subgroups by timepoint

We were deliberately broad in the protocol as it wasnâ€™t clear how conventional, or feasible, it was to study participants over multiple timepoints.

In addition, some studies reported change scores, as opposed to post-intervention outcomes. We are currently doing some reading on how to combine these statistics, but are currently dividing change score and post-intervention analyses into separate subgroups.

We collected data at timepoints baseline; post-intervention; change score; follow-up. However, some studies were excluded due to not reporting the statistics required to include in meta-analysis (e.g., sample size). When we look at the timepoints for each outcome, we see that post-intervention is the only timepoint routinely reported:
